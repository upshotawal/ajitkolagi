SHOOTERS' FUTSAL & GAME ZONE is one of the renowned Futsal ground of Bhaktapur situated at Suryabinayak, Bhaktapur which was established in 2072 B.S. SHOOTERS' FUTSAL & GAME ZONE is famous for it‟s well-facilitated sports venue for the customers. It has been providing facility of well-managed futsal ground with availability of all sport apparels. It has also favorable environment for trainings and organizing any tournaments. It‟s main mission is to establish itself as one of the leading sport venue in the field of delivering well-facilitated services to the sports enthusiasts.

BUSINESS PROCEDURE

SHOOTERS' FUTSAL & GAME ZONE uses manual system where the Futsal administrator records the data like: player‟s detail, booking details, trainings detail, tournament details, income, expenses, bills and other data into a log book. There is no use of any computerized system in Futsal Management.
